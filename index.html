Meet Eleanor

A safe space to be heard

Taking the first step towards therapy can feel daunting, but you don't have to face it alone. I provide a warm, non-judgemental environment where you can explore your thoughts and feelings at your own pace. Whatever you're going through — anxiety, relationship difficulties, past trauma or simply feeling stuck — I'm here to listen and to help.

My approach is tailored to you as an individual, drawing on integrative and evidence-based methods including CBT, person-centred therapy and EMDR. Whether you're seeking support with a specific issue or need a space to make sense of things, you'll find a compassionate, professional service here in Harrogate.
